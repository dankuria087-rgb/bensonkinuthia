About Benson

Benson Kinuthia is a committed leader who believes in development through inclusive governance. Born and raised in Murindat Ward, he understands the needs of the community and is passionate about empowering every citizen with access to quality services, resources, and opportunities. Benson brings fresh ideas and integrity to leadership.

Residents of Murindat face issues like unmaintained roads, insufficient health services, and low access to clean water — Benson is ready to change that.
Benson listens to the real voices of Murindat. He believes in participatory leadership that puts people first. ✅✅✅

My Manifesto

  • Water for All: Every home deserves access to clean, piped water — not promises.
  • Youth Empowerment: Tech skills, innovation hubs, and creative programs for the youth.
  • Reliable Roads: Upgrade key roads to all-weather standards and open rural access.
  • Smart Agriculture: Train farmers, subsidize inputs, and market produce beyond the ward.
  • Accessible Healthcare: Build and staff a fully-equipped dispensary in each sub-location.
  • Quality Education: ECDE classrooms, bursary support, and digital learning access for students.
  • Women & Girls: Support women groups with microfinance and promote girl-child rights.
  • Environmental Care: Tree planting, clean-ups, and zero-tolerance to illegal dumping.
  • Public Lighting: Light up marketplaces, paths, and trading centers for safety.
  • Ward Transparency: Publish all development projects and budgets publicly every quarter.
  • Community Participation: Establish monthly town hall forums to listen, act, and deliver.
